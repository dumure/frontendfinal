Frequently Asked Questions - FoodEx

What kind of cuisine does FoodEx offer?

FoodEx offers a wide range of cuisines, including traditional comfort foods, international dishes, and healthy options. Whether you're craving pizza, burgers, or a fresh salad, we have something for everyone.

Can I customize my meal?

Yes! At FoodEx, we believe in offering our customers the freedom to customize their meals. You can add or remove ingredients based on your preferences, dietary restrictions, or allergies.

Is there a vegan menu available?

Absolutely! We offer a variety of vegan options across our menu, including plant-based burgers, salads, and desserts. We make sure our vegan customers have plenty of delicious choices to enjoy.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes, FoodEx has a selection of gluten-free options. We offer gluten-free pizza crust, pasta, and other dishes to cater to those with dietary restrictions.

Can I order food for delivery?

Yes! We offer convenient delivery options through our website or popular food delivery apps. You can have your favorite FoodEx meals delivered straight to your door in no time.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for events, parties, and corporate gatherings. We can provide a variety of menu options tailored to your needs. Contact us for more details!

How can I join the FoodEx loyalty program?

Joining the FoodEx loyalty program is easy! Simply sign up on our website or in one of our restaurants to start earning rewards points with every purchase. Enjoy discounts, special offers, and more!
